ABOUT YO! BOWL

Yo! Bowl is a family-owned restaurant specializing in authentic mifen, a traditional rice noodle popular throughout Southwest China. Founded in Carrollton, Texas, in 2024, the restaurant combines traditional flavors with a customizable dining experience that allows guests to build their own bowls from a variety of broths, proteins, vegetables, and toppings.

Known for its naturally gluten-free rice noodles, slow-simmered broths, homemade yogurt, and select Japanese-influenced menu items, Yo! Bowl continues to introduce North Texas diners to one of China’s beloved regional foods in an approachable and modern way.
